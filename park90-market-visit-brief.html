The white space is a mid-luxury PDR-focused concept with retail integration: curated list, sommelier-led, private dining capable, without the exclusivity barrier of reservation-only.

Criteria Zone A — Thảo Điền Zone B — D3 Heritage Belt

Zone B (D3 Heritage) as Phase 2 or flagship if the brand story emphasizes Saigon heritage over contemporary lifestyle.
