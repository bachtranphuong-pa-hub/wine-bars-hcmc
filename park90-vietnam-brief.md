---

### **License Fee: 2.5% GTO → 2% GTO**

**Minimum:** $30k/year → $20k/year

**Why Lower:**
- Vietnam spending = 60% of Singapore
- $20k min = breakeven at $1M revenue (achievable)
- $30k min = requires $1.2M (too aggressive year 1)

**Escalator:** If revenue > $2M → increase to 2.5% GTO (upside sharing)

---

### **SKU Lock-In: 70% → 50%**

**Park90 Supply:** 50% of SKUs (premium/rare wines)  
**RCC Freedom:** 50% (entry-level, local importers)

**Why:**
- Vietnam import duty 50-70% → ex-SG wine expensive
- Local importers (Moët Hennessy, Vinifera) = better pricing
- Flexibility = faster response to Vietnam market preferences

**Quality Control:** RCC 50% subject to Park90 approval

---

### **Initial Inventory: $350k Full Payment → $250k Consignment**

**Consignment Model (12 Months):**
- Park90 invests $250k (owns inventory)
- RCC pays **only when wine sold**:
  - RCC keeps 40% of wine revenue
  - Park90 takes 60% (covers cost + margin)
- After 12 months: Transition to 60-day credit terms

**Why Critical:**
- Draft: $250k setup + $350k inventory = **$600k upfront** ❌
- Proposal: $150k setup + $0 inventory = **$150k upfront** ✅
- Risk-sharing: Park90 confident → should accept consignment

**Alternative (if rejected):** Reduce inventory to $200k, pay 50% upfront

---

### **Wine Pricing: Cost + Max 35% Markup**

**Problem:** Park90 earns 2% GTO + wine margin (double-dipping)

**Solution:** Transparency requirement
- Park90 discloses wine cost (ex-SG or ex-France)
- Maximum 35% markup on cost
- **Example:** $50 cost → $67.50 invoice → $135 retail
- RCC gross margin: 50-55% (healthy)

**Audit Rights:** RCC can verify cost annually

---

### **Ongoing Support: 1x → 2x Site Visits**

**Draft:** 1 annual visit + quarterly video training

**Proposal:** 2 annual visits + monthly video check-ins

**Why:**
- $20-30k/year license fee → needs more support
- Vietnam = new market → hands-on required
- Service quality = brand reputation

---

### **Early Termination (NEW)**

**Trigger:** EBITDA < $0 for 2 consecutive years  
**Notice:** 3 months  
**Refund:** Park90 returns 50% of setup fee ($75k)

**Why:** Downside protection (Park90 shares risk, not just upside)
