**Vietnam market reality:**
- Expected revenue: $1.2-1.8M/year (44 seats, high-end)

**License fee comparison:**

|---|---|---|---|

#### **Revised License Fee Structure**

**Base Rate:** **2% of GTO** (Gross Turnover)  
**Minimum:** **$20,000/year** ($5,000/quarter)

**Performance Escalator (Upside Sharing):**
- If revenue > $2M/year: Increase to **2.5% GTO**
- Rationale: At $2M+ = Park90 brand clearly driving success → deserve higher share

**Payment Terms:** Quarterly (vs. monthly in draft)
- **Why:** Cash flow smoothing (monthly = 12 payments, quarterly = 4)

- Moët Hennessy Vietnam

- The Warehouse (Bordeaux focus)
- Glouglou Wines (natural wine)

**Flexibility needed:** If Park90 ex-SG wine = 30% more expensive (duty + shipping), RCC needs freedom to source locally

#### **Revised SKU Structure**

**Park90 Supply:** **50% of total SKUs** (300-400 out of 600-800)

**Categories Park90 supplies:**
- ✅ Premium/rare wines (Burgundy, Napa, collectibles)
- ✅ Park90 signature selections (brand differentiators)
- ✅ Singapore member favorites (reciprocal program wines)

**RCC Freedom:** **50% sourcing** (300-400 SKUs)
- ✅ Entry-level wines (₫500k-1M/bottle, high volume)
- ✅ Italian/Spanish (better pricing from local importers)
- ✅ Vietnam-friendly regions (Rhône, Languedoc, Chile)

**Quality Control:** RCC-sourced 50% subject to Park90 approval (maintain brand standards)

### **2.4. Initial Inventory: $350k Full Payment → $250k Consignment (12 Months)**

#### **Rationale: Cash Flow Risk Mitigation**

**Park90 Draft:**
- Setup fee: $250k
- Wine inventory: $350k (full payment upfront)
- **Total cash out:** $600k BEFORE opening ❌

**Vietnam market risk:**
- Park90 Vietnam = first outlet (unproven format)
- $600k upfront = excessive risk for RCC

---

#### **Revised Structure: Consignment Model (12 Months)**

**How It Works:**

**Months 1-12 (Consignment Phase):**
1. **Park90 invests $250k inventory** (Park90 owns stock, not RCC)
2. **RCC pays only when wine sold** (revenue-sharing):
   - RCC keeps **40%** of wine revenue (cover service + opex)
   - Park90 takes **60%** (cover wine cost + margin)
3. **Example:**
   - Bottle retail: ₫5M ($200)
   - RCC revenue: **$80 (40%)**
   - Park90 revenue: **$120 (60%)** — covers $70 cost + $50 margin

**After 12 Months:**
- Transition to **60-day credit terms** (Park90 draft standard)
- RCC buys inventory going forward (proven demand)

---

#### **Why Consignment = Fair "Skin in the Game"**

✅ **Risk-sharing:** Park90 confident in product → should accept consignment  
✅ **Cash flow protection:** RCC saves $250k upfront (invest in fit-out/working capital)  
✅ **Proof of concept:** 12 months = test Vietnam market demand before RCC commits to inventory ownership

**Park90 Objection (likely):** "We're a licensor, not a bank."

**RCC Counter:** "Vietnam = new market for Park90. Consignment = proof you believe in your brand. If unwilling to share risk, why should we?"

---

#### **Alternative (If Park90 Refuses Consignment)**

**Option 1:** Reduce inventory to $200k (600 SKUs vs. 800)  
**Option 2:** Split payment: $100k upfront + $100k upon opening  
**Option 3:** Park90 provides vendor financing: $350k loan at 4% interest, 24-month term

### **2.5. Wine Pricing Transparency: Cost + Max 35% Markup**

#### **Rationale: Double-Dipping Concern**

**Park90 earns TWO ways:**
1. **License fee:** 2% GTO (based on RCC revenue)
2. **Wine margin:** Park90 = supplier (marks up wine cost → invoices RCC)

**Conflict of interest:**
- Park90 can markup wine 50-60% → RCC margin squeezed → lower retail sales
- BUT Park90 still gets 2% GTO regardless (revenue-based, not profit-based)
- **Result:** Park90 has NO incentive to keep wine prices low for RCC

---

#### **Revised Structure: Cost-Plus Pricing**

**Cost Disclosure:** Park90 must disclose wine cost (ex-SG or ex-France)

**Maximum Markup:** **35% on cost**

**Example:**
- Wine cost (ex-SG): $50/bottle
- Park90 invoice to RCC: **$67.50** (35% markup)
- RCC retail: $135-150 (2-2.2x, standard wine bar markup)
- **RCC gross margin:** 50-55% (healthy)

**Why 35% cap is fair:**
- ✅ Park90 earns supplier profit (35% margin on $250k inventory = $87.5k/year)
- ✅ RCC gets competitive pricing (can achieve 50%+ gross margin)
- ✅ Aligned incentives: Lower wine cost → higher retail sales → higher GTO revenue for Park90

**Audit Rights:** RCC can request proof of cost (supplier invoices) annually

---

**Cost to Park90:** +$5-8k/year (extra SG → SGN flights + hotel)  
**Value to RCC:** Quality assurance, brand consistency, Vietnam market insights
