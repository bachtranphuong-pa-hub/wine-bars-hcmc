Strategic Recommendations

🏆 Best Risk-Adjusted: Mid + Anantara

GDV $147M · Margin $50.1M (34%) · 15mo velocity

Fastest sell-out due to VN brand recognition. Minor Hotels procurement reduces construction cost vs. Capella. No price floor risk. Sweet spot between margin and speed.

💰 Best Margin: Mid + Zannier

GDV $147M · Margin $53.2M (36%) · 18mo velocity

Lowest fee structure (3% vs. 4%). Novelty premium — first Zannier in Ha Long = strong PR angle. Private Estates arm aligns perfectly with non-rental model. Buyer education cost is the tradeoff.

⚠️ Capella: Mid or Stretch only

Base scenario breaches Capella price floor (260tr < 280tr min). Mid scenario OK at $77M margin (30%). Capella commands highest perceived value but slowest velocity (24mo) and highest fitout cost ($3,000/m²).

Recommend Capella only if buyer demographic is pure UHNWI and velocity is not priority.
