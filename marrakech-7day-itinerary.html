🇲🇦 7-Day Marrakech & Atlas Itinerary

Culture • Mountains • Coast • Canyons • Local Cuisine

Day 1
📍 Marrakech
Arrival & Medina Immersion
  • Morning: Arrive, check-in Riad in Medina
  • Afternoon: Jemaa el-Fna Square, Koutoubia Mosque
  • Evening: Medina walk, sunset from rooftop
🍽️ Dinner: Nomad — Modern Moroccan, rooftop terrace
Day 2
📍 Marrakech
Culture Deep Dive
  • Morning: Bahia Palace, Saadian Tombs
  • Afternoon: Ben Youssef Madrasa, Souks (carpets, spices, leather)
  • Evening: Traditional hammam experience
🍽️ Dinner: Al Fassia — Traditional Moroccan, female-run kitchen
Day 3
📍 Ourika Valley
Atlas Mountains — Ourika Valley
🚗 Drive: 1.5 hours from Marrakech (60 km) — Scenic mountain roads
  • Morning: Drive to Ourika Valley
  • Midday: Setti Fatma Waterfalls hike (2-3 hours, moderate)
  • Lunch: Riverside restaurant in Setti Fatma village
  • Evening: Return to Marrakech
🍽️ Dinner: Le Jardin — Garden setting, Moroccan-Mediterranean fusion
Day 4
📍 Imlil / High Atlas
High Atlas — Toubkal Base
🚗 Drive: 1.5 hours from Marrakech (65 km) — Mountain roads to 1,740m altitude
  • Morning: Drive to Imlil Village (gateway to Mt. Toubkal)
  • Midday: Hike to Toubkal Base Camp viewpoint (3-4 hrs) OR easier trek to Armed village
  • Lunch: Mountain guesthouse (tagine with panoramic views)
  • Evening: Return to Marrakech
🍽️ Dinner: Pepe Nero — Italian (if you need a tagine break!)
Day 5
📍 Essaouira
Drive to Atlantic Coast
🚗 Drive: 3 hours from Marrakech (190 km) — Coastal highway, argan tree landscapes
  • Morning: Drive to Essaouira, check-in riad
  • Afternoon: Medina stroll, Ramparts walk, beach
  • Sunset: Harbor-side walk, watch fishing boats return
🍽️ Dinner: Chez Sam — Fresh seafood, harbor views (grilled sardines, calamari)
Day 6
📍 Paradise Valley / Taghazout
Canyon Oasis & Surf Town
🚗 Drive: 1.5 hours from Essaouira to Paradise Valley (100 km) — Coastal + inland mountain route
  • Morning: Drive to Paradise Valley (near Agadir)
  • Midday: Explore canyon, natural pools, cliff jumping (optional)
  • Afternoon: Taghazout beach town (surf vibe, chill cafés)
  • Evening: Drive back to Marrakech (3.5 hours via coast road)
🍽️ Dinner: Dar Moha — Riad dining, refined Moroccan cuisine
Day 7
📍 Marrakech
Leisure & Departure
  • Morning: Majorelle Garden, YSL Museum
  • Afternoon: Final souk shopping, traditional hammam spa
  • Evening: Departure or final night in Marrakech
🍽️ Farewell Dinner: La Mamounia — Legendary hotel, French-Moroccan fine dining (splurge!)
