Project Introduction — Royal Canary Corporation × ATC Advisors

From: Bach Tran, Deputy CEO · Royal Canary Corporation · April 2026

Dear Alan,

It was a genuine pleasure connecting, and we have been looking forward to following up with more detail on the opportunities we briefly touched on. After reviewing your background carefully, we believe there is a strong and natural alignment between your experience and the specific challenges we are navigating at Royal Canary Corporation right now.

Royal Canary Corporation (RCC) is a Vietnam-based hospitality and investment group with assets across luxury hotel operations, F&B, and development projects. Our portfolio includes the Le Méridien Saigon — a Marriott-managed luxury hotel in the heart of Ho Chi Minh City — alongside several development projects at various stages. We are at a significant inflection point, actively pursuing four strategic initiatives where we believe your network, experience, and judgment would add meaningful value.

The Four Areas We Would Like to Discuss

🏨 1 · Ultra Luxury Hospitality — Strategic Investment Advisory

We are in the early stages of evaluating a significant equity partnership opportunity at the HoldCo level with one of the world's most iconic ultra luxury hotel brands. This is a complex, high-stakes process that requires more than financial analysis — it demands a deep understanding of how these organisations are structured, how ownership and management relationships work in practice, and what it realistically takes for a new regional investor to be taken seriously at the table.

We are looking for an advisor who has been inside this world — someone who understands the dynamics from the owner's perspective, not just the brand's. We would value your guidance on deal positioning, the right approach to initial conversations, key questions to ask during due diligence, and how to present RCC credibly to this calibre of counterpart.

🏝 2 · Tuần Châu Island Resort — Brand Operator Search & Negotiation

We are developing a luxury resort project on Tuần Châu Island, Quảng Ninh — situated at the edge of Ha Long Bay, one of Vietnam's most iconic natural landscapes and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The project is designed as a low-rise, low-density luxury destination, and we are at the stage of identifying and securing the right international brand operator to anchor the hospitality component.

We are not looking for a list of names. We are looking for someone who can lead the process end-to-end — who knows the right people at the right brands, can open doors at the decision-making level, and has the credibility and experience to take conversations from first contact through to a signed term sheet. This is a primary mandate, and we would want you positioned as the lead negotiator on our behalf.

The brands we are most interested in exploring include Six Senses, Four Seasons, Anantara, Zannier Hotels, and similar positioning — though we remain open to the right strategic fit above all else.

🍽 3 · Le Méridien Saigon — F&B Operator Selection & Negotiation

We are seeking to significantly elevate the F&B offering at Le Méridien Saigon by bringing in a globally recognised restaurant brand or operator group. Our preference is for premium Asian dining concepts — operators with proven track records in international luxury hotel environments and a strong brand identity that resonates with both regional and international guests.

The groups we are most interested in include Tung Lok Group (Singapore's leading Chinese fine dining group), Hakkasan (internationally recognised Cantonese restaurant brand), Imperial Treasure (Singapore-based Michelin-starred Chinese restaurant group), and Sunset Hospitality (Dubai-based, operators of globally recognised lifestyle F&B concepts). We would welcome your assessment of fit and your ability to facilitate introductions and early-stage conversations at the principal level.

We are realistic about the complexity of bringing an international operator into Vietnam for the first time, and we would value your candid perspective on which groups are most likely to be receptive and what a workable deal structure might look like.

🍷 4 · Park90 @ Saigon — Wine Advocacy & Brand Building

We are bringing the Park90 concept to Saigon in partnership with Park90 (SG) Pte Ltd — the Singapore-born fine wine bar known for curating wines rated 90 points and above by Robert Parker Wine Advocate, and for offering labels such as Domaine de la Romanée-Conti and Château Pétrus by the glass. This will be the brand's first Vietnam outpost, following successful locations at Conrad Singapore Orchard, The St. Regis Kuala Lumpur, and Park Hyatt Bangkok.

We are looking for a credible, well-connected voice in the luxury wine space to serve as a Wine Advocate and Brand Ambassador for Park90 @ Saigon. This is not a purely ceremonial role — we are looking for someone who can contribute meaningfully to wine curation direction, anchor events and masterclasses, create content that reaches the right audience, and help build genuine brand awareness within the UHNW and luxury lifestyle community across Vietnam and the broader SEA region.

How We See the Partnership Working

We envision a structured advisory engagement covering all four areas, with clear scope, regular communication, and mutual accountability. We are committed to treating this as a genuine partnership — providing you with the context, access, and decision-making support you need to be effective, and respecting your time and expertise accordingly.

We would be grateful if you could share your thoughts on each of the four areas — including where you see the strongest fit, how you would approach each engagement, your proposed terms, and any questions or considerations you would like to discuss before moving forward.

We look forward to a candid and productive conversation.

Bach Tran Deputy CEO, Royal Canary Corporation
