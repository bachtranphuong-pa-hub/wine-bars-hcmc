✓ Why Nguyen Van Huong Works

  • Parallel to Xuan Thuy = 30-40% lower rent but same neighborhood
  • Heritage shophouses available (authentic wine bar vibe)
  • Walking distance to MAD, Lửa, Grotto (proven wine bar demand)
  • Residential clusters = repeat customers (expats + affluent Vietnamese)
  • Quieter than Xuan Thuy = better for PDR privacy, less street noise
  • Retail integration = street-facing ground floor, walk-in traffic from Thao Dien Village
  • International school proximity = expat families (daytime retail, weekend brunch)
  • Parking solutions = nearby street parking, valet partnerships possible
  • Easy access = Xuan Thuy, Quoc Huong main roads within 200-300m
  • Lower competition = No wine bar on Nguyen Van Huong yet (first-mover)

⚠ Challenges & Mitigations

  • Parking limited → Mitigation: Valet service, partnerships with nearby buildings
  • Heritage preservation rules → Mitigation: Work with architect experienced in heritage renovations
  • High competitor density (MAD, Lửa, Grotto nearby) → Mitigation: PDR focus differentiates, corporate market untapped
  • Foot traffic lower than Xuan Thuy main strip → Mitigation: Destination positioning (reservations, events), not walk-in dependent
  • Lease negotiation = heritage shophouses = family-owned (slower process) → Mitigation: Start broker engagement early (3-6 months lead time)
