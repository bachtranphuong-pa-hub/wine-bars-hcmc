## 🔄 DIVIDEND REINVESTMENT STRATEGY (DRIP)

### **Manual DRIP (Vietnam doesn't have automatic DRIP):**

**How to Maximize Returns:**
1. **Collect cash dividends** (Jan, May, Jun, Aug)
2. **Reinvest immediately:** Buy more shares of same stock (or highest yield stock)
3. **Compounding effect:** More shares = more dividends next year

**Example (NT2):**
- **2026:** Own 100 shares NT2 @ ₫21,500 = ₫2,150,000 invested
- **Dividend:** ₫1,500/share × 100 = ₫150,000 cash
- **Reinvest:** Buy 7 more shares @ ₫21,500 (₫150,000 / ₫21,500)
- **2027:** Own 107 shares → dividend ₫1,600 × 107 = ₫171,200 (14% more)
- **2028:** Reinvest again → own 115 shares → dividend ₫184,000
- **3-year total:** ₫505,200 dividends (vs. ₫480,000 without reinvesting) = +5.3% bonus
