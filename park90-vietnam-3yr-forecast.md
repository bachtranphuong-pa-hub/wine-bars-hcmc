- Year 1: Ramp-up (68% avg utilization)
- Year 2: Base case = stable 100%, Optimistic = 110%

**Cost Evolution:**
- COGS improve due to scale (42% → 35-33% wine, 32% → 25-23% food)

**External Factors:**
- Vietnam wine market grows 10-15%/year (industry trend)

**1. Year 1 Ramp-Up Slower Than Expected (30% risk)**

- Mitigation: Focus local Vietnam market, not depend on Singapore

**5. Competition from New Openings (15% risk)**
