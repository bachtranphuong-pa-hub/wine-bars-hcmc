RCC acquires 100% of Jen TC through a newly incorporated SPV (Vietnamese LLC).

  • Enable cleaner future capital raising or exit
  • Simplify ownership structure for Marriott brand agreement purposes

2.3   Shareholder Loan Treatment (Critical)
⚠️
This is the most complex part of the deal. Total loans outstanding ~VND 398 billion from two sellers.
Lender Amount Terms Treatment Options
Jen Capital (BVI) — Working capital loan VND 148.4B
(USD 5.7M)		 Interest-free; due Nov 2027 Option A: Novate to RCC SPV at USD/VND spot
Option B: Sellers retain + repay from sale proceeds
Jen Capital — 10% convertible loan VND 8.2B
(USD 0.3M)		 10% p.a. compound quarterly; due Nov 2027; accrued interest VND 31.5B Must be retired or renegotiated
Dao Ngoc — Land acquisition loan VND 137.2B Interest-free; due Nov 2027 Option A: Convert to equity credit in SPA
Option B: Forgive as part of total consideration
Dao Ngoc — Other payable VND 42.0B — Fold into land acquisition consideration
Recommended Approach
  • Jen Capital loans (USD-denominated): Sellers retire/repay from equity sale proceeds. RCC SPV takes a clean balance sheet. This reduces RCC's FX exposure.
  • Dao Ngoc loan (VND 137.2B + VND 42B = VND 179.2B): Restructure as deferred payment or offset against land transfer obligations. Tie release to LURC transfer completion.
  • Net economic effect: Total consideration for RCC = Purchase Price + assumption/retirement of Dao Ngoc obligations. Estimated all-in acquisition cost: VND 250–270 billion (equity + Dao Ngoc obligation settlement).

2.5   Seller Warranties & Indemnities
Key warranties to require from both Jen Capital and Dao Ngoc:
  1. Title warranty: Sellers warrant full and unencumbered title to Jen TC shares
  2. LURC warranty: Dao Ngoc warrants completion of LURC transfer within 6 months; penalty = holdback forfeiture
  3. T&H Ha Long warranty: No current claims, proceedings, or enforceable orders against Jen TC's specific LURCs or land rights; sellers indemnify RCC for any loss arising from T&H Ha Long proceedings affecting Jen TC
  4. Tax warranty: All taxes up to closing date properly filed; FCT exposure indemnified by sellers up to EY's quantified VND 15.5B
  5. Social housing warranty: T&H Ha Long (master developer) bears all social housing obligations; sellers to provide written confirmation from T&H or indemnify RCC if obligation falls on Jen TC
  6. Funding warranty: No adverse change in MBBank facility terms; sellers to disclose any conditions precedent not yet satisfied
  7. No material adverse change: No new material contracts, encumbrances, or obligations created between signing and closing
