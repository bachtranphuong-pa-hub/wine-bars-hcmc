Section 02 — Recommendation

Category Priority Ranking for LMSG

🎯 RCC Category Recommendation (Zera Score Order)

  • #1 Wellness Service (4.1/5): Highest strategic score. Madison House-style private club or Barbaard grooming lounge both fill a genuine market gap and have natural banya synergy. Barbaard is fastest to deploy (Vietnam-native). Madison House is highest impact but requires space and SHG negotiation.
  • #2 Premium Japanese (3.8/5): Strongest guest alignment and LM DNA fit. Japanese cuisine is the most universally loved premium category and LMSG has no Japanese anchor. Risk: supply chain complexity + rising competition (Nobu established, Zuma incoming).
  • #3 Premium Western (3.7/5): Fastest path to revenue — Park90 wine bar already active at BARSON with term sheet in negotiation. Bottle locker membership creates recurring revenue. A Hawksmoor-caliber steakhouse is also absent from D1.
  • #4 Modern Premium Chinese (3.6/5): Strongest group dining revenue ceiling but most competitive category in D1 HCMC. Execution quality is the differentiator. Hakkasan or Ling Ling bring proven international brand equity.
  • #5 Modern Premium Indian (2.8/5): Highest white space but weakest overall fit for LMSG's guest mix. A medium-term consideration if HCMC's Indian expat and Indian-tourism segment grows. Not recommended as near-term priority.
  • Recommended Phase 1 Portfolio: Activate Premium Western (Park90 / BARSON — immediate) + Wellness Service (Barbaard — fast follow). Then evaluate Premium Japanese as the major F&B anchor for the next available space.
