Market Read: HCMC's upscale Chinese dining is dominated by hotel-anchored Cantonese (Shang Palace, Li Bai, Long Triều) — the same model Tung Lok operates in Singapore. The standalone premium format (Dim Tu Tac) captures volume but not margin. White space: A contemporary Cantonese concept in D1 or D3 with strong private dining capacity, modern interior, and a serious dim sum lunch program targeting the corporate segment — currently underserved above Dim Tu Tac but below Long Triều's price ceiling.
