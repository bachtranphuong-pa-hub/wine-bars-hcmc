CHINESE RESTAURANT CONCEPT — LE MÉRIDIEN SAIGON

Market Analysis & Brand Positioning Report  |  March 2026  |  Confidential  |  3C Tôn Đức Thắng, Q.1, HCMC  |  Sources: Google Drive (Brands + Floor Plans) + Public Research

Executive Summary

Le Méridien Saigon sits at the highest-density 5-star hotel + Grade A office corridor in HCMC (Tôn Đức Thắng, Ben Nghé). Five Chinese restaurant concepts were reviewed from the brand shortlist (Hakkasan, Ling Ling, Imperial Treasure, Taste Paradise, TungLok), alongside competitive mapping of all Chinese restaurants within 1km. A clear market gap exists: no competitor on Tôn Đức Thắng offers dedicated VIP/private dining rooms for Chinese cuisine, and no contemporary Chinese concept with cocktail bar integration exists within 1km.


Floor plans confirm the Level 3 (3-Meal Restaurant, ~600–800m²) as the optimal conversion space — full kitchen infrastructure, direct lobby access, structural grid supporting private room subdivision. The Club Floor Royal Club (298m²/64 pax) is a secondary premium-VIP option.


Recommended positioning: "Contemporary Cantonese Business Club" — ₫500k–1.2M/pax, 3–4 VIP rooms, targeting in-building office tenants (13,972m² Grade A) + hotel corporate guests. Best brand fit: Hakkasan (management/license) or Imperial Treasure (management model launching 2026). Taste Paradise (franchise) is viable if lower capex is required. Ling Ling requires trophy nightlife venue — not optimal. TungLok is financially distressed — partnership possible but brand risk.

Final Recommendation Summary

Positioning: Contemporary Cantonese Business Club

1
Convert Level 3 (3-Meal Restaurant) — full kitchen, 600–800m², lobby access. Subdivide with 3–4 VIP rooms (8.4m bay grid)
2
Price ₫500k–1.2M/pax — fills gap between Kabin (₫550k ceiling) and Long Triều (₫2M floor)
3
Target in-building office workers (captive 1,000–1,400 pax), hotel corporate guests, private business dinners
4
Saturday–Sunday Yum Cha 10:00–14:30 to capture weekend walk-in (Crystal Jade hotel barrier = opportunity)

Brand Priority

1
Hakkasan — highest brand prestige, management model, no SEA competition, matches 5★ hotel requirement. Approach London/Dubai office.
2
Imperial Treasure — 8 Michelin Stars, management model launching 2026, Vietnam as new market, cluster model synergy. Approach Singapore HQ.
3
Taste Paradise — franchise option if lower capex required. Established SOP, chef sourcing, 6,000sqft requirement matches Level 3.
4
Avoid Ling Ling (nightlife DNA mismatch) and caution on TungLok (financial risk) unless deep discount on management fees justifies.
