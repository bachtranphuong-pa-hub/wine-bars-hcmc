🎯 Bottom Line for Anh Quang

  • The opportunity is real — Park90 model is proven in SG, KL, Taipei, Niseko. Being first in Vietnam is a genuine differentiator.
  • The price is steep — USD 250K setup + USD 300–500K inventory = VND 14–20B upfront. This is NOT a low-risk/low-capex play as previously framed.
  • Venue conflict is the wildcard — Park90 explicitly wants a private shop-lot/villa, not a hotel lobby. Either Park90 is flexible (clarify NOW), or this becomes a standalone venue decision with different economics.
  • Recommendation: If Park90 is flexible on venue → negotiate hard on setup fee and inventory phasing → proceed with Bar only (defer Le Cave). If Park90 insists on standalone → present as a separate RCC F&B venture, not a BARSON add-on.
  • Le Cave defer: USD 500K additional inventory for retail is a separate, sizeable bet. Recommend Phase 2 only after Bar proven in Year 1.
