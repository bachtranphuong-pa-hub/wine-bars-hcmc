Section 08

Legal Risk Summary — 4 Closing Conditions

All 4 conditions are Bên Bán's responsibility to resolve before RCC pays any tranche. These are not RCC's risk to absorb — they are contractual preconditions.

1. GCN QSDĐ Update — 138m² Discrepancy
Land use certificate must be updated to reflect correct area (15,966m²) and transferred to Jen Tuần Châu. May trigger additional land-use fees from tax authority.
HIGH
2. Construction Permit Transition (LXD 2025)
Project exempted from building permit under LXD 2014. Must confirm transitional validity under LXD 2025 or obtain new permit. No commencement notice before 1/1/2026 creates risk. If unresolvable: RCC self-files, deducts ~1–2B from price.
HIGH
3. Infrastructure Agreement with T&H Hạ Long
Shared infrastructure (17,311m² road/utilities) owned by T&H — must formalize usage agreement, handover timeline, and maintenance fee structure before Closing.
HIGH
4. PCCC Fire Safety Sign-Off
Fire safety inspection required for shared infrastructure area. Expected resolution procedural — lower complexity than other CPs.
MEDIUM
