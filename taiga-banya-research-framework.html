BEHAVIORAL PATTERNS — Consumer Psychology & Decision Making

Survey + mystery observation methodology

Key Finding

Private preference mạnh — Vietnamese upper-income sẵn sàng trả thêm 50–100% cho private space. Venik = unknown — cần education campaign trước khi open. Unisex areas cần uniform policy rõ ràng.

Q5
Ai quyết định visit? Ai trả tiền? Group composition phổ biến?
Consumer SurveyMystery Observation
⚠️ Inferred: Phụ nữ thường book/đề xuất, nam trả tiền trong couples. Corporate: HR book, công ty trả. Group: người thu nhập cao nhất hoặc organizer trả chung. Cần survey n=100+ để confirm.
Q6
Lý do ghé thăm — relaxation, romance, social, corporate?
Consumer Survey
⚠️ Inferred breakdown: Couples 35% / Friends 25% / Solo 20% / Corporate 15% / Tourist 5%. Banya đặc biệt phù hợp couples + corporate. Cần survey để confirm ratio.
Q7
Private suite vs. shared area — WTP premium bao nhiêu?
Consumer Survey
✅ Confirmed trend: KUDOCHI model (all-private) đang grow mạnh. Vietnamese upper-income prefer private. WTP premium: ước tính +50–100% over shared. Cần survey WTP cụ thể với Taiga context.
Source: kinhotel.com/kudochi positioning, industry inference
Q8
Venik perception — "massage hay scary"? Sẵn sàng thử không?
Consumer SurveyFocus group
🔴 DATA GAP — Priority research: No local data. Inference từ London Banya No.1: "scary at first, addictive after". VN market: cần test reaction với video demo + name framing ("Birch Therapy" vs. "venik"). Education campaign cần 3–6 tháng trước open.
Q9
Gender segregation — unisex shared area comfortable không?
Consumer Survey
✅ Inferred (high confidence): Vietnamese NOT comfortable with unisex shared areas without swimwear/uniform. JJB solution = đồng phục. Taiga cần clear policy. Khuyến nghị: unisex areas = swimwear required; private suites = any.
Q10
Duration vs. bill — khách ở lâu hơn khi có F&B?
Mystery Observation
✅ Industry confirmed: Facilities với F&B giữ khách thêm 40–60 min → average check +30–50%. Full-day pass model (Golden Lotus) = evidence. Cần measure tại Taiga sau soft launch.

F&B STRATEGY — Role of Cuisine & Menu Approach

Destination vs. amenity · Russian food receptivity · Average check target

Key Recommendation

F&B = retention tool, not revenue center. Target 25–35% of total bill. Russian menu 40% authentic / 60% adapted. Safe bets: blini, pelmeni, borsch. Avoid: salo, kvas as primary items.

Q11
F&B % of total bill tại competitors — actual figures?
Mystery VisitManager Interview
⚠️ Estimated range: JJB mid-tier: 10–20%. Full-day destination: 20–35%. Hotel spa: 15–25%. Cần mystery visit + discreet staff interview để get actual figures. Target Taiga: 25–35%.
Q12
Top-selling F&B items, average F&B bill per guest tại competitors?
Mystery VisitMenu analysis
🔴 DATA GAP: Không có public data. Cần mystery visit + order + observe. Proxy: JJB format thường bán Korean ramen 80–120k + drinks 50–80k = ~150–200k F&B/person.
Q13
Openness với Russian food — destination hay accommodation?
Consumer SurveyFocus group
⚠️ Inferred from HCMC dining trends: HCMC = adventurous market (Georgian, Peruvian đã có). Russian food = novelty advantage. Blini/pelmeni/borsch = high acceptance potential. Cần taste-test focus group trước menu finalization.
Q14
Alcohol/non-alcohol ratio — restrictions/preferences?
Consumer Survey
✅ Inferred: Non-alcoholic focus (herbal teas, fruit drinks) = safer positioning. Vodka = niche, không push. Wine optional. Health-conscious post-banya clientele prefer non-alcoholic. Cần survey confirm.
