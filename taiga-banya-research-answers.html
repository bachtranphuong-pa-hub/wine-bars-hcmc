Q2.5
How comfortable is the target audience with shared unisex areas in swimwear/clothing vs. strict male/female separation?
Answer HIGH CONFIDENCE — cultural baseline clear
Vietnamese consumers (middle and upper income) are not comfortable with unisex shared thermal areas without proper clothing, particularly in groups that include people they don't know. This is a strong cultural norm, not a niche preference.
JJB solution: Golden Lotus and all Korean jjimjilbang in HCMC solve this with a mandatory uniform (provided on entry). The uniform neutralizes discomfort — guests feel clothed and equal. High acceptance rate once uniform is on.

Recommended menu adaptation ratio: 40% Russian authentic / 60% adapted

— 40% Russian authentic: Blini, pelmeni, borsch, piroshki, smoked salmon, Russian herbal teas, kvass (as curiosity item)
— 60% adapted: Asian-friendly soups, lighter protein options, fruit-based drinks, familiar desserts with Russian-inspired names
