Positioning & Management Direction

Tuần Châu Private Estate Collection — An Exclusive Enclave for Vietnam's Most Discerning Families

The Tuần Châu Private Estate Collection is conceived as Vietnam's first ultra-luxury, non-rental private estate enclave — 18 individually crafted villas (800–1,000m²) set within 3 hectares of curated landscape on Tuần Châu Island, overlooking Hạ Long Bay. Each villa is designed as a permanent private sanctuary: not a hotel, not an investment product, but a family legacy home.


The target owner is a Hanoi or Haiphong-based ultra-high-net-worth family (net worth USD 20M+), seeking a private retreat for personal use, multi-generational family gatherings, and discreet corporate entertainment — not rental income. The operative word is exclusivity: no strangers, no check-in desks, no public access. Ownership here is membership in a closed community of 18 families who share a world-class landscape and a white-glove estate management service.


The management model is Private Estate Management — not hotel operations. A dedicated estate team (resident manager, butler corps, maintenance, culinary concierge) serves owners on-demand, with the brand's service philosophy embedded in every interaction. The estate operator does not manage occupancy, RevPAR, or yield — they manage readiness, personalization, and seamless owner experience at all times.
