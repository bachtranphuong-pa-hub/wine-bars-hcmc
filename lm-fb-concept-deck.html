05 · Recommendation
Strategic Direction

Primary: Concept A (Jade Duck House) × TungLok Group

Why this combination wins: Clearest white space capture, strongest external traffic draw, and most practical operator pathway. TungLok already operates in Vietnam (Hà Nội), has a standalone Peking Duck concept ready for export, and is actively seeking SEA expansion opportunities.

The curved facade provides the ideal stage for Peking Duck's theatrical tableside carving — a viral, memorable moment that no Q1 competitor currently offers. Weekend dim sum brunch anchors recurring local traffic. Private rooms with CNY/corporate banquet packages drive high-margin seasonal peaks.

Secondary: If TungLok timeline is slow, approach Siji Minfu via Singapore team — Vietnam as second international market after RWS Sentosa 2026 is a compelling pitch.

01
Feasibility (4 weeks)
Confirm CAD area, MEP requirements (duck roasting ventilation, grease trap), structural load capacity. Commission F&B feasibility study.
02
Operator Outreach (M1–2)
Contact TungLok Vietnam (Hà Nội) — warm intro. Parallel: Siji Minfu Singapore expansion team. NDA + concept brief sharing.
03
Term Sheet (M3–4)
Negotiate Management Agreement or JV. Key terms: mgmt fee 8–12% revenue, capex split, operator min guarantee, branding rights.
04
Design + Build (M5–18)
Appoint F&B interior designer (operator-approved). Kitchen engineering, FF&E spec. Target opening: 12–15 months from approval. Capex: $550–700K.
